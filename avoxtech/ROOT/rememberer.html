Rememberer

Rememberer is a small Android app for reminders.
From the Google Play Store:

Let Rememberer keep track of all those little things you need to remember but don't want cluttering up your calendar!

Rememberer has flexible and easy to use rules so you can quickly setup almost any reminder you need. Rules like "make lunch for tomorrow" Sunday through Thursday night at 8pm, and "take out the garbage" every second Wednesday at 7:30am. Rememberer reminds you with notifications and optional voice-to-text.

Visit the Google Play Store to download.
