Skins

What is a skin?

A skin is a background image that you can use to spice up Pink Calendar.
You can select an bitmap image to display as the background in the main calendar and another for the day planner.

Notes for using skins with Pink Calendar:

  1. Performance: drawing the day planner with a background image may slow your system a bit.

  2. Colors: depending on the image and text colors choosen, it may be difficult to read the day planner entries.
    Generally, try to use images which are either light or dark and of similiar colors, for example mostly reds.
    Systems using 256 or fewer colors may not display the background images correctly.

  3. Sizing: it is up to you to make sure the image fits. I could force it to fit (or tile it), but that wouldn't look very good.
    You can get imaging software from places like     TuCows or Download.com to help with this.

  4. You may need to convert your images from gifs or jpegs to bitmaps (use the imaging software for this).
