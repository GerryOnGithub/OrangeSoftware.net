Synchronize with Google calendar 


 If you have a gmail account you can now sync Pink Calendar with Google Calendar!
 Please read these important notes:
  • You need to have .Net 2.0 installed (it is probably already on your system). If you need it you can get it here.
  • To sync: from PinkCal select File, then Backup, then Synchronize
  • You'll be asked for your gmail username and password
  • PinkCal will sync about 30 days worth of data at a time (started from yesterday up to 30 days in the future).
