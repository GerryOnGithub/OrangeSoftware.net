what PinkCal does not do

  1. Pink Calendar does not "network" - PinkCal is meant for home and small business users.
     
  2. Pink Calendar is not as flexible as programs like Microsoft Schedule or Netscape Calendar. I don't have legions of programmers working on PinkCal and my program is intended to be small, fast and simple.
